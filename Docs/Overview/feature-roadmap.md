# Feature Roadmap

### **2.2 Dashboard**

* High-level overview of platform activity
* Campaign summaries and performance snapshots
* Quick access to key modules
* Recent activity feed

---

### **2.3 Unified Multi-Channel Inbox**

* Central inbox for all customer conversations
* Supported channels:

  * Email
  * WhatsApp
  * Telegram
  * Twitter/X
  * In-app chat
* Real-time message updates
* Conversation filtering and tagging

---

### **2.4 Campaign Management**

* Create, edit, and manage email campaigns
* Campaign scheduling and sending
* Campaign performance tracking
* Folder-based campaign organization

---

### **2.5 Visual Template & Design System**

* Prebuilt email templates (admin-managed)
* Custom visual template builder (Canva-like)
* Drag-and-drop components
* Template saving and reuse

---

### **2.6 Contacts & Audience Management (CRM)**

* Contact creation and management
* CSV import and bulk upload
* Custom contact fields
* Contact segmentation and filtering

---

### **2.7 Automation & Workflow Engine**

* Drag-and-drop automation builder
* Time-based triggers
* Event-based triggers
* Conditional logic and actions

---

### **2.8 Website Builder**

* Website creation and management
* Prebuilt themes
* Drag-and-drop content editing
* Website analytics

---

### **2.9 File & Brand Asset Manager**

* Central cloud storage for assets
* Upload and manage images and files
* Reuse assets across campaigns, templates, and websites

---

### **2.10 Analytics & Reporting**

* Email performance analytics
* Automation performance insights
* Website analytics
* User engagement tracking

---

### **2.11 A/B Testing**

* Campaign A/B testing
* Subject line testing
* Content variation testing

---

### **2.12 Social Messaging & Bulk Communication**

* Bulk messaging to prior social chat users
* Channel-based message targeting

---

### **2.13 Publishing & Subscriptions**

* Newsletter creation
* Blog / RSS publishing
* Subscriber management

---

### **2.14 Help & Support System**

* Help center / documentation
* Support articles
* In-app chat support

---

### **2.15 Settings & Configuration**

* Profile and account settings
* Team and permission management
* Integration settings
* API keys and third-party configurations

---

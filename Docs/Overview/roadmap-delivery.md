# **ROADMAP & DELIVERY PLAN (12 WEEKS)**

---

## **1. Purpose of This Document**

This document outlines the **proposed 12-week delivery roadmap** for the Campaign & Growth Management SaaS platform.

The roadmap is structured into **clear phases with defined outcomes**, while allowing room for iteration, validation, and third‑party integration dependencies.

---

## **2. Delivery Philosophy**

* Phased, milestone-driven development
* Iterative delivery with continuous validation
* Feature completeness prioritized over premature optimization
* Flexibility for refinement based on feedback and integrations

---

## **3. Overall Timeline Summary**

* **Total Duration:** 12 Weeks
* **Delivery Style:** Phased milestones
* **Validation:** End-of-phase reviews
* **Dependencies:** Timely design handoff and third‑party API access

---

## **4. Phase Breakdown**

---

### **Phase 1 — Foundation & Core Platform (Weeks 1–3)**

**Focus:** Platform groundwork and access control

**Key Deliverables:**

* Project setup and repository structure
* Supabase backend initialization
* Authentication (signup, login)
* Organization / workspace model
* Team members and role-based access
* Base dashboard layout
* Initial database schema (core entities)

**Outcome:**
A secure, multi-tenant platform foundation ready for feature development.

---

### **Phase 2 — Core Growth Systems (Weeks 4–6)**

**Focus:** Contacts, campaigns, and assets

**Key Deliverables:**

* Contacts & CRM module (CSV import, custom fields)
* File & brand asset manager
* Campaign creation flow
* Email template selection
* Amazon SES integration
* Campaign scheduling and sending
* Basic campaign analytics

**Outcome:**
Users can create audiences and send functional email campaigns.

---

### **Phase 3 — Builders & Communication Layer (Weeks 7–9)**

**Focus:** Visual builders and real-time communication

**Key Deliverables:**

* Custom Canva-like email template builder (MVP)
* Prebuilt template system
* Unified inbox (email, WhatsApp, Telegram, in-app chat)
* Real-time messaging via Supabase Realtime
* Website builder framework (themes + pages)

**Outcome:**
Users gain visual design power and centralized customer communication.

---

### **Phase 4 — Automation, Monetization & Optimization (Weeks 10–12)**

**Focus:** Intelligence, scale, and polish

**Key Deliverables:**

* Automation workflow builder
* Event-driven triggers and actions
* A/B testing module
* RSS / newsletter & blog system
* Social bulk messaging
* Razorpay subscription & billing
* Advanced analytics views
* Final UI/UX refinement and QA

**Outcome:**
A production-ready, monetized SaaS platform.

---

## **5. Milestones & Reviews**

* End of Phase 1: Core platform review
* End of Phase 2: Campaign system validation
* End of Phase 3: Builder & inbox usability review
* End of Phase 4: Final acceptance & launch readiness

---

## **6. Assumptions & Dependencies**

* Timely delivery of UI/UX designs by the client
* Access to required third-party APIs (SES, WhatsApp, Razorpay, etc.)
* Feedback provided within agreed review windows

Minor timeline adjustments may occur due to validation cycles, third-party approvals, or integration constraints.

---

## **7. Roadmap Status**

✅ **Roadmap approved for execution.**

Any major scope changes will require timeline reassessment.
